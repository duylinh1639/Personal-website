Designing the Systems That Power Modern Buildings

With over 12 years of experience in MEP engineering, I specialize in the design and coordination of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems for complex building projects. My expertise spans from initial concept design through construction administration.

I am passionate about creating energy-efficient, sustainable building systems that reduce environmental impact while delivering superior comfort and performance. I hold a Professional Engineer (PE) license and am LEED Accredited Professional.

Throughout my career, I've led multidisciplinary teams on projects ranging from high-rise office towers and healthcare facilities to data centers and educational campuses. I believe in leveraging BIM technology and collaborative design processes to deliver projects on time and within budget.
